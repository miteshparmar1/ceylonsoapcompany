Soaps and Shampoo Bars

Our soaps and shampoo bars contain no preservatives, no parabens and no sodium lauryl sulphate (SLS), and/or sodium laurate sulphate (SLES). About Us

Our History

Ceylon Soap Company is located in the picturesque hills of the Central Province - the brain child of Samantha Helm who came up with the idea of 100% natural, handmade Sri Lankan soap whilst contemplating a complete change in her life from being a London based chartered insurance broker to “manager” of her family’s coconut plantation in Sri Lanka. Samantha after having spent 17 years in the UK made the decision to return to home to take over the management of her family’s Estate (handed down from her maternal grandfather). It was all about continuing family traditions and keeping a venture that employs many local people for two generations a continuous operation.

Our Ethos

The ethos of The Ceylon Soap Company is to be a sustainable endeavour making products that are handmade, 100% natural, vegan, not tested on animals and as Sri Lankan as possible giving the end user a unique and luxury bathing experience. The manufacturing is kept simple with small batches to ensure that our processes are green and environmentally safe. The ingredients are sourced as locally as possible from our estate, Sri Lanka and Asia in order to keep our carbon footprint small.

Our manufacturing is done at our estate, which is located between two rural villages (Bandarapola and Kiula) as is the case with most parts of Sri Lanka youth unemployment is a huge socio-economic problem. We hope that with the growth of The Ceylon Soap Company business we will be able to provide employment to many more local people and continue the family tradition of being and continuing to be a positive influence in the local community.
